We solve the system:

$$
\begin{aligned}
2x + 3y &= 12 \\
x - y &= 1
\end{aligned}
$$

From the second equation:

$$
x - y = 1
$$

$$
x = 1 + y
$$

Substitute into the first equation:

$$
2(1+y) + 3y = 12
$$

$$
2 + 2y + 3y = 12
$$

$$
2 + 5y = 12
$$

$$
5y = 10
$$

$$
y = 2
$$

Now compute:

$$
x = 1 + y = 1 + 2 = 3
$$

**Answer:**

$$
x = 3, \quad y = 2
$$
